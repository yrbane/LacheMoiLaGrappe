Tired of spam calls?

Tired of people calling you during dinner to sell you insurance you don't need, fake training programs, or solar panels when you live in a studio apartment?

It's over. LacheMoiLaGrappe is an anti-spam shield for your Android phone.

SMART FILTERING:

- ARCEP telemarketer blocking: 17 telemarketing-reserved prefixes are automatically blocked
- Unknown number filtering: Not in your contacts? No ringing
- Hidden number blocking: Those who hide behind a private number can deal with it
- Auto SMS: Polite reply to unknown callers asking them to identify themselves (opt-in)
- Allowlist / Blocklist: Dedicated screen to manage your lists
- Custom prefixes: Add your own prefixes to block

POLISHED UX:

- Guided onboarding for first-time setup
- History with search, swipe-to-action and CSV export
- Home screen widget to track blocked calls
- Grouped and non-intrusive notifications
- Haptic feedback on actions

PRIVACY FIRST:

- Zero data collection: everything stays on your phone
- Encrypted database (SQLCipher AES-256)
- No Internet permission: nothing leaves your phone
- No server, no tracking, no ads
- Open source: code is public on GitHub
- Free: no subscription, no premium version

Because I'd rather have people who care about me on the line, not people who care about my wallet.
